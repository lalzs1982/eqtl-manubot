Consistence of eQTL effect on associated gene expression across tissues for HP eQTL and control SNPs
Proportion of major eQTL effect (increase or decrease) on gene expression for HP eQLT and control SNPs across 18 tissues was shown.
1, HP eQTL tend to deviate from 0.5 very much compared against control SNP, which means more consistence of expression changing effect for HP eQTL;

2,  the control should be around 0.5 as expectation, but there still could be some true eQTL in the control SNPs, so some deviation observed. 
Regression coefficients between eQTL and associated gene expression was used to determine different changing effect of increase (>0), decrease (<0) when regression p < 0.01 or uncertain effect when regression p > 0.01 in each tissue, and then the number of  tissues with increase or decrease effects in the 18 tissues obtained, proportion for the major effect was calculated as Deviation or Consistence of changing effects here (Note: 0.5 was  random expectation).

Enrichment of 3'UTR HP eQTL in functional elements
Odds ratio of 3'UTR HP eQTL enrichment within RBP binding regions, miRNA binding sites (seed match plus 10 bp flanking region), m6A sites (m6A and the 2 bp flanking region), polyadenylation signal sites was show by comparison agaist control SNPs. Each dot represent OR of each tissue, and the vertical line for 95% confidence interval. Red star above each line represent significance level of enrichment by fisher's exact test (* for <0.05, ** for <0.01 and *** for < 0.001). 
1, significant enrichment of HP eQTL within RBP binding regions, m6A sites, polyadnylation signal sites, and slightly enrichment within miRNA binding sites observed; 
1, RBP binding regions was obtained from POSTAR database, and selected tissues of HEK293,HeLa, HepG2, K562 which contain binding sites for many more RBPs was used. binding regions >50 bp truncated to be 50 bp, as a result on average # binding regions for each of  # RBPs analyzed here; Allele-specific RBP binding sites was downloade from published work by #, 7489 binding sites for 147 RBPs in 2 cell lines : HepG2 and K562 obtained and used here. 

2, # miRNA binding sites for # miRNAs  from targetScan database (conserved predictions) downloaded, and 10 bp extended around the seed match used here for enrichment analysis;

3, 477453 m6A sites was obtained from RMBase database, 2bp flanking each m6a site that may affect the modification was analyzed here;

4, 35316 polyadenylaton signal sites in 3'UTR that may affect polyadenylation was obtained from the UTRdb.
./res.hp_eqtl_reg_enrichment_OR.pdf
